Welcome to RugzWithLuv

Rugzwithluv is an online store based out of San Luis Obispo and Los Angeles. This small business commissions and sells custom rugs made entirely by hand with “luv”. Rugzwithluv not only strives to make unique products for their customers but also works to build their community on love and support. The rugzwithluv brand welcomes all and consistently donates a portion of their proceeds to a cause that advocates for the arts in low-income communities.
